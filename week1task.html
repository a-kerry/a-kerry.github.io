Why i chose this topic?


The following Paragraph from an article whilst doing my inital research capatavated my interest in the topic:

As a variety of new digital media channels have emerged such as video, music, podcasts, and writing. Creators can now connect with audiences worldwide and generate revenue. Currently, the creator economy is Valued at $14 billion per year, generated from these creators and digital channels. As technological change is rapid, creativity is seen as uniquely human and less vulnerable to the influence of technology and is critical for humans in the future (Cremer, D,2023).
